PRODUCTS & SERVICES

LANCETECH LIMITED supply all types of laboratory materials, equipments, and analytical instruments for pharmaceutical companies, biochemistry labs, microbiology labs, research laboratory, university research labs, food & beverages, testing labs, textiles.

We supply all types of chromatography columns
IC Columns. GC Columns. HPLC and UHPLC Columns. Autosampler Vials and Closures. Chromatography Autosampler Well Plates and Accessories.
We also supply
All consumables lab items. Chemical Reference standard (USP, BP and EP). Used instruments in UK and worldwide with services. Spare parts of different machines used in pharmaceuticals. All types of analytical and measuring items for QC, QA and research lab.
